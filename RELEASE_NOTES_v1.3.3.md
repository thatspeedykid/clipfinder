## 🔤 Burn Subtitles — Beta

A brand new sub-tab inside the **Transcript** tab. Burn captions directly onto your videos.

> **⚠ This feature is in active beta.** Timing and styling may not be perfect on all videos. More improvements are coming — report any issues to [@MarsScumbags](https://x.com/MarsScumbags).

**What's included:**
- Built-in transcription — no need to visit the Transcript tab first, transcribe right from the subtitle tab
- Auto-transcribes with word-level timestamps for accurate sync
- Pause detection — subtitles go blank during silence gaps instead of running ahead
- **Karaoke mode** — each word highlights as it's spoken (yellow or custom color)
- Font picker, size, bold/italic, ALL CAPS
- Text color, outline color + thickness, background box with opacity
- 3×3 position grid (top/middle/bottom, left/center/right)
- 5 style presets: Standard, Karaoke, Cinematic, Minimal, **TikTok** (3 words at a time)
- Live preview — grabs a real frame from your video with your style applied
- Output folder picker, saves as `VideoName - Subtitled - ClipFinder.mp4`

## ⚡ Tweet Generator — 3 Options

Hit Generate once and get **3 completely different tweet options** in one shot:

- **Option 1** — Drama account style: bold headline, "The tea:", "The take:", specific quotes from transcript
- **Option 2** — Viral/meme energy: short, chaotic, screenshot-worthy
- **Option 3** — Thread opener: 240-280 chars, hook that drives replies and clicks

**Smarter hashtags:**
- Uses actual names from your context field as hashtags
- Never generates `#gaming` or `#gamingscandal` unless the clip is literally about gameplay
- Each option gets its own hashtags matching what that specific tweet says
